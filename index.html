Sonnet in the Shape of a Potted Christmas Tree Related Poem Content Details BY GEORGE STARBUCK

* O fury- bedecked! O glitter-torn!

Let the wild wind erect bonbonbonanzas;  junipers affect frostyfreeze turbans;    iciclestuff adorn all cuckolded creation in a madcap crown of horn!

It’s a new day; no scapegrace of a sect tidying up the ashtrays playing Daughter-in-Law Elect; bells! bibelots! popsicle cigars! shatter the glassware! a son born  now   now

while ox and ass and infant lie together as poor creatures will

and tears of her exertion still cling in the spent girl’s eye and a great firework in the sky drifts to the western hill.
